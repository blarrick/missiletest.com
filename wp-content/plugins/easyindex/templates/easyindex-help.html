Getting started with EasyIndex

Here's a few tips to get you started. There's more documentation over on our support site. If you've got any questions have a look through the Knowledge Base there. If you can't find what you're looking for, you can leave us a support ticket and we should be able to help. You can also create a support ticket directly from the EasyIndex Settings Page.

Your first index

Here's the basic steps you follow to create an index. If you are unsure of how it all works, we'd suggest you create a trial index using the defaults and then modify the parameters to get it just how you want.
If you aren't sure of what a particular setting is, click on the "i" icon, which is displayed next to every field, to open an explanation in a new window
  • On the admin menu on the left, go to Indexes and click on Add new
  • Enter the title of your index and the index slug (the slug will form part of the index's URL)
  • Fill in the parameters for the Primary and Secondary indexs, or just use the defaults. You can read more about Primary and Secondary indexes here
  • Underneath the index's main settings, select what it is you want to index
  • Once you've entered the index's settings, you should click on "Generate Thumbnails". You can read more about that here
  • You're ready to Publish and view your index!
